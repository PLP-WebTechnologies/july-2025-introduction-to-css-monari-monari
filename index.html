Delicious Strawbery Milkshake

A creamy and refreshing treat you can whip up in minutes!

    • Ingredients
    • Instructions
    • Tips

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ½ cups milk (cold)
  • ¼ cup fresh strawberries
  • Whipped cream (for topping)
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place ice cream, milk, and fresh strawberries into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into tall glasses.
  4. Top with whipped cream and more fresh stawberries.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips

    For a thicker milkshake, use less milk. Add caramel or bananas for extra flavour.
